Jizan


Introduction

Jizan is a city in the Asir region of Saudi Arabia.

Jazan has a hot desert climate (Köppen: BWh) with an average annual temperature above 30 °C (86 °F). The weather is very hot all year round and its average annual temperature is one of the hottest in the world, even in its short winters. It is very humid but parched in some months, making the weather very oppressive.
